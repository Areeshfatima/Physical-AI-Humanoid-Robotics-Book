# Introduction to Physical AI & Humanoid Robotics

Welcome to the comprehensive textbook on **Physical AI & Humanoid Robotics**. This educational resource provides an in-depth exploration of the intersection between artificial intelligence and robotics, with a specific focus on humanoid robot systems.

## About This Textbook

This textbook is designed for students, researchers, and professionals interested in understanding the fundamental principles and advanced concepts of physical AI and humanoid robotics. Through this course, you will learn about:

- The theoretical foundations of physical AI
- Core robotics concepts including perception, planning, and control
- Advanced topics in humanoid robot design and functionality
- Real-world applications and case studies
- Integration of vision-language-action models in robotic systems

## Learning Path

This textbook is organized into four comprehensive modules:

1. **Module 1 - ROS2 Fundamentals**: Learn the core concepts of Robot Operating System 2 (ROS2), the standard framework for robotics applications.
2. **Module 2 - Digital Twin & Simulation**: Explore Gazebo and Unity-based simulation environments for robot development and testing.
3. **Module 3 - Isaac Sim & Navigation**: Understand NVIDIA Isaac Sim for advanced simulation and navigation systems.
4. **Module 4 - Vision-Language-Action Systems**: Delve into multimodal AI systems that integrate vision, language, and robotic action.

## Getting Started

To get the most out of this textbook:

- Read each module sequentially to build foundational knowledge
- Follow the practical exercises and examples
- Explore the additional resources provided in each section
- Engage with the community through the GitHub repository

Begin your journey by selecting a module from the sidebar, starting with Module 1 - ROS2 Fundamentals to establish a solid foundation in robotics software frameworks.
