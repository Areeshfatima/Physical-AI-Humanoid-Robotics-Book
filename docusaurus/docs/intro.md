# Introduction

Welcome to the Physical AI & Humanoid Robotics Textbook. This comprehensive guide explores the latest developments in robotics and artificial intelligence, focusing specifically on humanoid robots and their applications.

In this textbook, you will learn about:
- Fundamentals of physical AI
- Design principles for humanoid robots
- Control systems and locomotion
- Human-robot interaction
- Applications in various fields

Let's begin our journey into the fascinating world of humanoid robotics.
